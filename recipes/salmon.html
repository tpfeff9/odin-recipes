Salmon Recipe

Description

A simple, delicious recipe for baked salmon. Healthy and savory, the salmon can be used in numerous combinations: in a salad, as some sliders, or paired with rice or quinoa and some veggies in a bowl.

Ingredients

  • Center cut of salmon
  • Lemon
  • Garlic Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Preaheat Oven at 400
  2. Use a glass baking dish, and set some aluminum foil on the bottom
  3. Drizzle some olive oil on the foil
  4. Place the salmon in the baking dish, on top of the foil
  5. Cut your lemon in half
  6. Take one half of the lemon, and squeeze the juice on the salmon
  7. Springle some garlic salt, italian seasoning, and parmesan cheese onto the salmon
  8. Cut the other half of the lemon into thin slices, then place the slices onto the salmon
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes
